Action Mapping — Every Alert Type → CTA

No alert exists without a resolution path. If we can't offer an action, we don't show the alert.

Alert CategoryProblemCTA ActionAPI Call
Crawl Failure Banks unreachable POST /api/crawl/{bank_id}
Crawl Failure Anti-bot blocked POST /api/crawl/{bank_id}?bypass=proxy
Rate Anomaly Sudden rate spike/drop POST /api/crawl/{bank_id} (re-crawl to confirm)
Data Quality Low completeness POST /api/parse/{bank_id}
Stale Data Not crawled recently POST /api/crawl/{bank_id}
Strategy Health Low success rate POST /api/learn/{bank_id}
